Job Description

SharePoint Site Creator

During my work term at the University of Guelph as a SharePoint Site Creator within the School of Computer Science, I was responsible for designing and managing an internal SharePoint repository for teaching resources. This included researching institutional policies, consulting with faculty to determine their needs, and creating and posting relevant content on the SharePoint sites. Notably, I developed and implemented 7 unique SharePoint sites, all of which are currently in use by staff and students. The role required various skills, including experience with computer systems, file permissions, some web programming, and strong communication skills. While some of these skills were learned in class, others, particularly those related to SharePoint and web-based tools, were acquired and refined on the job.

Time Management

Objective: Improve personal organization and time management skills using GitLab as a productivity tool.

Action Plan:

Log time spent on tasks using GitLab’s time tracking features, set estimates, and generate reports to compare estimated vs. actual time.

Create separate tickets for various project aspects, use labels and milestones for task categorization and prioritization, and explore GitLab boards for visual management.

Integrate GitLab with calendar applications for deadline management and configure notifications for task deadlines and milestones.

Practice sprint planning and conduct daily stand-ups for effective time management by planning ahead.

Reflection:

Reflecting on my journey to enhance personal organization and time management through GitLab, I am satisfied with the progress made. Implementing sprint planning and daily stand-ups has helped me adopt a more structured approach to managing tasks and deadlines. The use of GitLab’s project tools, including separate tickets, labels, and milestones, has greatly improved my task categorization and prioritization. Additionally, GitLab’s time tracking and calendar integrations have reduced missed deadlines and improved task management, benefiting both my work and personal projects, such as my project car.
